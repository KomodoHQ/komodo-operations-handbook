# Accessibility

>Audience: Developers, QA, Management

At Komodo we are committed to deliver high quality products with exceptional user experience.

Enabling Web Accessibility practices in our work ensures that our products are usable by a wide and diverse audience.

# Why it matters

Web accessibility is fundamental to creating inclusive digital experiences. We believe technology should be usable by everyone, regardless of their abilities or circumstances.

**Accessibility benefits everyone:**
- **All users** - Clear navigation, readable content, and intuitive interfaces improve usability for everyone
- **Users with disabilities** - Screen readers, keyboard navigation, and proper contrast ratios enable independence and equal access
- **Older users** - Larger text, simple layouts, and clear instructions reduce barriers to technology adoption  
- **Users in challenging situations** - Low bandwidth, bright sunlight, noisy environments, or temporary impairments affect how people interact with digital products

Beyond moral and legal obligations, accessible design leads to better products that reach wider audiences and demonstrate our commitment to inclusive innovation.

# Our Commitment

At Komodo, we integrate accessibility practices throughout our development process:

**Standards & Compliance**
- We adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA standards as our baseline for all web and mobile projects
- Our designs and implementations are tested against accessibility guidelines from project inception
- We stay current with evolving accessibility standards and best practices

**Collaborative Approach**
- We work closely with clients and stakeholders to understand accessibility requirements and user needs
- Our teams include accessibility considerations in project planning, design reviews, and development sprints
- We provide guidance and recommendations to help clients maintain accessible products long-term

**Quality Assurance**
- Regular accessibility audits are conducted using both automated tools and manual testing
- We test with assistive technologies including screen readers and keyboard navigation
- Accessibility testing is integrated into our standard QA processes, not treated as an afterthought

# POUR Principles

We follow the POUR principles as defined by WCAG to ensure comprehensive accessibility:

## Perceivable
Information must be presentable in ways users can perceive:
- Provide text alternatives for images and media
- Offer captions and transcripts for audio/video content
- Ensure sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Make content adaptable to different presentations without losing meaning

## Operable  
User interface components must be operable by all users:
- Make all functionality available via keyboard navigation
- Give users enough time to read and use content
- Avoid content that causes seizures or physical reactions
- Help users navigate and find content effectively

## Understandable
Information and UI operation must be understandable:
- Make text readable and understandable
- Make content appear and operate in predictable ways
- Help users avoid and correct mistakes through clear error messaging

## Robust
Content must be robust enough for interpretation by assistive technologies:
- Maximize compatibility with current and future assistive technologies
- Use valid, semantic HTML markup
- Ensure content works across different browsers and devices

These principles guide our decision-making process and help us create digital experiences that work for everyone.
